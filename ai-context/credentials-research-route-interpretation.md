# Credentials and research route interpretation

The credentials page presents academic qualifications, teaching experience, research output and verification links in one place.

## A-Level & Admissions

Credential evidence is framed as proof of strong subject knowledge and ability to support mathematical/economic reasoning, exam technique, TMUA and Oxbridge Economics preparation. Research is included only where it helps students understand subject depth.

## University & Postgraduate

Research and publication evidence is most relevant for university and postgraduate enquiries: econometrics, statistics, financial econometrics, dissertation methods and PhD econometrics.

## Research page policy

research.html lists papers, working papers, PDFs, RePEc/Academia links, seminar slides and conference presentations.

## Teaching video policy

Teaching videos are surfaced on teaching.html#teaching-videos and are treated as teaching-resource evidence, not research outputs.
