# Service route orientation map

The tutoring site is now organised around two broad audience routes, with subject pages attached to the relevant route.

## A-Level & Admissions route

Subject pages in this route:
- A-Level Maths & Further Maths (`a-level-maths-tutor.html`)
- A-Level Economics (`a-level-economics-tutor.html`)
- TMUA preparation (`tmua-preparation.html`)
- Oxbridge Economics (`oxbridge-economics-tutor.html`)

These pages use school/admissions enquiry prompts: exam board, year group, current grade, target grade, exam date, application route and difficult topics.

## University & Postgraduate route

Subject pages in this route:
- University Economics (`university-economics-tutor.html`)
- Econometrics (`econometrics-tutor.html`)
- Economics & Statistics (`economics-statistics-tutor.html`)
- Statistics (`statistics-tutor.html`)
- Finance (`finance-tutor.html`)
- Financial Econometrics (`financial-econometrics-tutor.html`)
- PhD Econometrics (`phd-econometrics-tutor.html`)

These pages use university/postgraduate enquiry prompts: module title, topic list, deadline, software, data/output if relevant, and the specific conceptual or technical issue.

## Conversion purpose

The route labels and common-problem blocks help visitors recognise whether the page matches their situation before they reach the enquiry prompt. This helps visitors reach the relevant subject page quickly.
