# Pricing and resources route split

The site uses two main audience routes for conversion and navigation:

1. A-Level & Admissions: A-Level Maths, Further Maths, A-Level Economics, TMUA and Oxbridge Economics.
2. University & Postgraduate: university economics, econometrics, statistics, finance, financial econometrics, dissertation methods and PhD econometrics.

Pricing wording: the rate is confirmed before any paid session is booked. Sessions are booked per session, with no upfront commitment.

Resources are grouped by route on blog.html and teaching.html. Technical econometrics/time-series resources are labelled as University & Postgraduate study notes. Some downloadable teaching materials are historic notes written for previous teaching and do not represent current courses or current course content.
