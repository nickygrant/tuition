# Answer capsules and claim precision

Key pages contain short visible summaries that help visitors confirm whether the page matches their level, subject and goal.

Claim wording is intentionally precise:

- Cambridge BA, MPhil and PhD in Economics.
- University teaching experience.
- Published economics/econometrics research.
- External academic profiles for identity evidence.
- Publication, PDF and seminar links for subject-expertise evidence.
- Tutoring pages remain the main student-facing routes.

The study-note library includes board-specific A-Level exam guides (A-Level Maths, Further Maths and Economics for OCR, Edexcel and AQA). Each states its specification code (e.g. Edexcel 9MA0, OCR H240, AQA 7357) and summarises paper structure, assessment objectives and weightings, common mark losses from examiner reports, and original worked exam-style questions. Per-page capsules are in answer-capsules.json.
