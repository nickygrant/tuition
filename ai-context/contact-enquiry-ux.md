# Contact and enquiry UX map

The site uses two broad enquiry routes before subject selection.

## A-Level & Admissions
Ask users to send exam board, year group, current grade, target grade, exam date, admissions deadline if relevant, and difficult topics.

## University & Postgraduate
Ask users to send module title, degree level, topic list, deadline, software or dataset if relevant, and the specific point they are stuck on.

## Booking policy
The consultation is free. The rate is confirmed before any paid session is booked. Sessions are booked per session with no upfront commitment.
