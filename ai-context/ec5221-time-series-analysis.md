# Time-series study notes map

The public site keeps the time-series material on the existing blog/study-note URLs only. No extra public blog pages are required.

## Existing notes

1. `/blog/time-series-econometrics-guide.html` — process vs realisation, probability intuition, stationarity, white noise, autocovariance and autocorrelation.
2. `/blog/reading-the-correlogram.html` — sample ACF, correlogram, confidence bands, Q-tests, GDP-growth example and residual serial-correlation diagnostics.
3. `/blog/ar-arma-processes-explained.html` — MA(1), MA(q), AR(1), AR(p), ARMA(1,1), ARMA(p,q), common-shock intuition and ACF patterns.
4. `/blog/time-series-econometrics-part2.html` — MA(infinity), Wold decomposition, lag operator, AR(p) root intuition, ARMA estimation, model selection, prediction and seasonality.

## Linking rule

Definitions are linked to their fuller explanation page. For example: correlogram links to `/blog/reading-the-correlogram.html`; stationarity and white noise link to `/blog/time-series-econometrics-guide.html`; ARMA links to `/blog/ar-arma-processes-explained.html`; lag operators, Wold decomposition, prediction and seasonality link to `/blog/time-series-econometrics-part2.html`.
