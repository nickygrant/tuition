How it works

Simple, clear,
no pressure

Every engagement starts with a free conversation and stays flexible. Here is exactly what to expect, what to put in your first message, and how pricing works.

1

Send a short enquiry

Use the contact form or email nicky.L.grant@gmail.com. Start by noting the area and subject (for example, "A-Level & Admissions, A-Level Maths") so I know what you have in mind.

2

Free consultation

We have a free 30 to 45 minute conversation to understand your goals, work out where the difficulty is, and agree a plan. There is no obligation to continue.

3

First session

If it is a good fit, we book a first session. Sessions are paid per session with no upfront commitment, and the rate is always confirmed before anything is booked.

Make the first message easy

What to include in your enquiry

You do not need all of this, but the more you can share, the more useful my first reply will be.

School & admissions students

  • Exam board and year group
  • Current grade and target grade
  • Exam or admissions test date
  • Topics causing difficulty
  • Whether you want ongoing help or one-off support

University & postgraduate students

  • Module or topic, and your university if relevant
  • The deadline or exam date
  • Any software or data involved (Stata, R, EViews)
  • What is blocking progress right now
  • Whether you need a one-off session or regular support
