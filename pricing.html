How it works · Pricing

Sessions, programmes
& pricing

There is no one-size-fits-all programme. A student preparing for an A-Level exam needs something different from a university student working through an econometrics problem set. The consultation is used to diagnose the need, propose a plan and confirm the per-session rate before any paid session is booked.

The process

How it works,
step by step

1

Free initial consultation, 30-45 minutes

A video call to understand where you are, what you're working towards, your exam board if relevant, and how you learn best. I ask diagnostic questions. At the end I propose a plan, topics, session frequency, likely duration. No charge. No commitment to continue.

2

Agree a plan and schedule sessions

If the consultation goes well and you would like to proceed, we agree the next session and likely focus. Sessions are booked per session. A regular weekly or fortnightly pattern is possible, but there are no upfront commitments.

3

Sessions via video call with shared whiteboard

Each session is conducted on Zoom, Teams, or Google Meet, your choice, with a shared digital whiteboard for working through mathematics, proofs, and code. This format is excellent for quantitative subjects. Sessions can be recorded if both parties agree.

4

Adjust at any point

There is no minimum commitment. Sessions are booked per session, so you can pause, change frequency or shift topics as your situation changes.
