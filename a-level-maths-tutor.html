A-Level Mathematics

Pure, Statistics and Mechanics

  • Algebra, functions and graph transformations
  • Sequences, series and the binomial expansion
  • Trigonometry, identities and equations
  • Exponentials, logarithms and modelling
  • Differentiation, integration and differential equations
  • Vectors and coordinate geometry
  • Statistics: data, probability, distributions and hypothesis testing
  • Mechanics: kinematics, forces, Newton's laws and moments

A-Level Further Mathematics

Core Pure plus optional applied modules

  • Complex numbers and Argand diagrams
  • Matrices and linear transformations
  • Proof by induction and series
  • Roots of polynomials and the method of differences
  • Hyperbolic functions and further calculus
  • Polar coordinates, further vectors and planes
  • Differential equations and Maclaurin series
  • Optional Further Statistics, Further Mechanics or Decision Maths

Also strong preparation for the TMUA and for the mathematical reasoning needed in Oxbridge economics applications.
