Interactive Exam Questions
Edexcel A-Level Maths

Ten exam-style questions, worked one line at a time. Open a question, and each line is revealed only after you commit to the previous one — just like building a solution under exam conditions. You're then marked the way Edexcel marks it: method (M) and accuracy (A) marks adding up to the question's real total, with feedback on every slip.

1 · Open a questionEach is an Edexcel-style question with its real mark total. Try it on paper first.
2 · Build the solutionPick each line; the next appears once you commit. Wrong options mirror real, common mistakes.
3 · Get your marksPress Mark to see your score out of the total, line-by-line M1/A1 awards and feedback.

Marks follow Edexcel conventions: M = method mark (correct approach), A = accuracy mark (correct result; needs the method), dM = a method mark that depends on the previous one. Each question shows its total, exactly as on the paper.
