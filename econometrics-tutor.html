Why choose Dr Grant

The difference between
general tuition and specialist support

Published research in econometric theory
Dr Grant has published peer-reviewed work in econometric theory through CeMMAP and the Institute for Fiscal Studies, co-authored with Professor Richard J. Smith at Cambridge. He works at the frontier, not just teaching what is in textbooks.
Designed the modules, not just taught them
At Manchester and St Andrews, Dr Grant did not just deliver lectures, he designed entire econometrics courses from scratch, wrote problem sets, and marked exams. He knows exactly what universities are testing and why.
Intuition first, then formalism
The biggest problem in econometrics teaching is going straight to matrices and proofs before the student understands what the method is actually doing. Every session starts with the economic and statistical intuition.
All levels, genuinely
Most tutors can handle introductory OLS. Dr Grant can handle it too, and also the GMM estimation in your PhD thesis, the weak instrument problem in your Masters dissertation, or the cointegration analysis in your final-year project.
