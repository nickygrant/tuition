Background & CV

Who you'll be working with

Dr Nicky Grant is a Cambridge-trained economist who tutors A-Level Maths, Further Maths and Economics, prepares students for the TMUA and Oxbridge Economics applications, and supports university and postgraduate students in economics, econometrics, statistics and finance. Alongside more than twelve years of university teaching, this includes experience working in a secondary school and safeguarding training, so younger students and their parents are in experienced, trusted hands.
